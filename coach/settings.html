2 Optional Customisation
All optional. The bot uses smart defaults if you leave anything blank. Fill in what you know and skip the rest.
1 of 12
What do you do?
Describe your service in plain English. The bot uses this when leads ask what you offer.
What do people get when they work with you?
List the specific deliverables or things included. Bullet points work well here.
Who is this for?
Be specific. The bot uses this to qualify leads and spot a good fit early.
How does working with you actually work?
Walk through the steps from first contact to being a client. Helps the bot explain the process when leads ask.
What's the main result you help people get?
The headline outcome - what someone's life looks like after working with you.
Who are your best-fit leads?
The type of person who gets the best results and is easiest to convert. The bot uses this to spot warm leads early.
Who is NOT a good fit?
Leads the bot should gently filter out or not push hard to close.
What objections do you usually get, and how do you handle them?
The bot will use this to respond naturally when leads push back.
When should the bot send the booking link?
Tell the bot exactly when to offer the link, and when not to. The more specific the better.
Why should someone act now rather than later?
What usually triggers a lead to finally commit? The bot can reference this naturally in conversation.
What builds trust with your leads?
Proof points, guarantees, or things that make you stand out. The bot mentions these when leads seem hesitant.
Anything else the bot should know?
FAQ, how you work, policies, anything that comes up regularly in DMs.
