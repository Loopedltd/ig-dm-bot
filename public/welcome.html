Add the Call to Your Calendar

After booking on Calendly, add the event to your personal calendar so you don't miss it.

📅 Google Calendar

  1. Open the confirmation email you received from Calendly after booking.
  2. Click the "Add to Google Calendar" link in the email. It opens Google Calendar automatically.
  3. Review the event details and click "Save" to add it to your calendar.
  4. Make sure notifications are turned on so you get a reminder before the call.

🍎 Apple Calendar (iPhone / Mac)

  1. Open the confirmation email from Calendly on your iPhone or Mac.
  2. Tap or click the "Add to Apple Calendar" link. Your Calendar app will open.
  3. Tap "Add" to confirm. The event will appear in your calendar immediately.
  4. Alternatively, open the .ics file attached to the email and tap "Add to Calendar" when prompted.
